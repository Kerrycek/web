About vpsFree.cz, z.s.

vpsFree.cz is association founded in 2008 to host virtual servers (VPS) for its members. Nonprofit asociation means that every member has right to participate and influence its run. It is in contrast to commercial hostings where every customer have to respect hard rules or leave.

When asociation member see some things which could be done better or change he can get involved. His activity is welcomed. It doesn't mean that companies or another organisations can't join us. It is very convenient for them. We do mutual job here and we don't want to make profit on each other.

Organiztion has been founded as direct reaction to inflexible services and exorbitant prices of commercial hostins in Czech and ever worse situation in Slovak. Therefore we are trying to run our servers with more care than commercial providers and also with maximum transparency.
