About vpsFree.cz, z.s.

vpsFree.cz is a non-profit association founded in 2008 to host virtual servers (VPS) for its members. Non-profit association means that every member has right to participate and influence how it's run. It is in a contrast to commercial hostings where every customer has to respect hard rules or leave.

When our member sees some things which could be done better or changed, he can get involved. His activity is welcomed. It doesn't mean that companies or another organisations can't join us. It is very convenient for them too. We do mutual job here and we don't want to make profit on each other.

Our Organization has been founded as a direct reaction to inflexible services and exorbitant prices of commercial hostings in Czech Republic and ever worse situation in Slovak Republic. Therefore we are trying to run our servers with more care than commercial providers and also with maximum transparency.
