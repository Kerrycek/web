Common questions

I forgot my login/password for vpsAdmin, what can I do?

Just contact our support and use the e-mail address registered in vpsAdmin. We will reset your password.

I want to run Windows server or another system on my VPS. Is it possible?

It is possible if you will enable full virtualization with KVM. We provide detailed manual.

I need more than one VPS. Is it possible?

Of course. Just contact our support.

I don't fit in with RAM or HDD. Can I get more of it?

VPS can be only doubled or trippled together with the membership fee. So you can have 8 GB RAM and 240 GB HDD or 12 GB RAM and 360 GB HDD. Or you can increase HDD space for 8 euro cents/GB.

It is possible to get an invoice?

Please send your billing information to our support and we will create invoice for you. You have to ask us every time, just reply on reminder from vpsAdmin.

Can you set me a reverse record for IP addresses?

Contact support and we will manage it. You will find more info in our PTR manual.

What domain should I use in my reverse record?

Read our manual about reverse records and manual for mail server on our knowledge base.

I need to extend life of my Playground VPS, is it possible?

Contact our support please.

I want to have my own kernel in VPS, is it possible?

We use container technology OpenVZ. It means that every user share one kernel. If you really need own kernel, you have to use full KVM virtualitazion.

Can I run Docker?

Yes, you can make it work using detailed instructions.

Can I have a swap file?

We can do it only if you have to install Oracle database and only during installation time. Oracle needs swap only over installation and it is not checking it ever after. If you need temporary swap, please contact support.

My VPS is not working, what can I do?

Subscribe to our outage-list which contains all info about planned and unplanned outages.

What if I will be late with payment?

In that case please contact our support and we will handle it. It is really important to write us soon to avoid sspend your membership.

Can I be member as a company?

Yes, it is possible. Just fill registration form for companies and include name of responsible person who will manage VPS and who we can contact. Contact support if you need invoice.
